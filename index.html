Why: new clients who view proposals and interview within the first 7 days more likely to convert to make their first hire by far. ATS on the mobile app will drive new client conversion by encouraging viewing proposals and interviews in the critical first 7 days.

What: We designed and launched an MVP applicant tracking system. Talking to our desired small business hiring manager, we understood that they are busy but hiring is important. They only have small time windows in between meetings, on their commute or on their couch after the kids are asleep. So they need hiring to be broken down into bite sized chunks. The key tasks in ATS included viewing and organizing proposals. The most promising proposals could enter an interview process. You can make an offer to the freelancer you want to hire.

Measure: The key behavior we want to drive was viewing proposals and ultimately hire a qualified freelancer. We saw new clients who engaged on the app be 4x more likely to hire within the first 7 days of signing up.
