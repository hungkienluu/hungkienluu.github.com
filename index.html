Hung K. Luu

Product Manager · San Francisco, CA

I am a product manager - an intersection of technology, business and customer insight. Cliche you say? But what I mean by that is that I have experience with all 3 areas and in applying all 3 all at once in the role of Product Manager.

What makes me unique is that I’m a full stack PM who can dive into a technical issue with an engineer, design a data event taxonomy with an analyst, collaborate with a designer to explore design directions and tell stories to align executive leadership. Because I can work at all altitudes, I get things done. But more importantly, I solve customer problems and deliver business impact for my teams.
